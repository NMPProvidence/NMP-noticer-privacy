Privacy Policy for Noticer

Effective Date: July 1, 2025

App: Noticer (note-taking app)

1. Data Collection

Noticer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users. All notes created in the app are stored locally on the user's device in a secure SQLite database. The app does not use any cloud synchronization, account registration, or data tracking.

If users enter personal information into their notes (e.g., names, emails, health or contact data), it remains on the device and is entirely under the user's control and responsibility.

2. Backups

Users can manually export backups of their notes to the device’s Documents folder. Backups are saved as password-protected ZIP files containing the local database. The developer has no access to these files or any user data.

3. Advertising

Noticer displays non-personalized ads using Google AdMob. These ads are based on general, contextual information (such as the app category or language settings), not user data.

Google AdMob may use limited technical data such as:

  • Device type and language
  • App usage (e.g., number of opens)
  • Approximate location (e.g., country)

Noticer does not allow personalized advertising or user profiling. This helps ensure compliance with privacy regulations such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

For more about non-personalized ads, see Google's AdMob privacy documentation.

4. Age Restriction

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. It does not knowingly target minors or collect data from them. If you are under 13, please do not use this app or enter any personal information.

By using the app, you confirm that you are at least 13 years old.

5. Responsibility and Consent

Users are fully responsible for any personal content they enter into the app and how they choose to store or share it. The developer does not access or process any note content.
