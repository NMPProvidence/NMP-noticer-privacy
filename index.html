Privacy Policy for NMP Noticer

Effective Date: January 15, 2026

Application: NMP Noticer (note-taking app)

1. Data Collection

NMP Noticer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage data. The application works entirely offline.

All notes created in the app are stored locally on the user’s device in a private SQLite database that is not accessible to other applications.

The app does not use:

  • advertising or ad networks
  • analytics or tracking tools
  • cloud services or data synchronization
  • user accounts or registration
  • subscriptions or in-app payments

2. Note Content and User Responsibility

Any information entered into notes (including personal or sensitive data such as names, email addresses, health information, or passwords) remains entirely on the user’s device.

The developer does not have access to note content and does not process it in any way. Users are fully responsible for the content they store in the app.

3. Password Protection

Users may protect selected notes with a password. Passwords are stored locally on the user’s device and are used solely to secure access to notes within the application.

Passwords are never transmitted, shared, or sent outside the device and are not accessible to the developer or any third parties.

If a password is lost, the protected note cannot be recovered.

4. Backups

Users may manually create backups of their notes on their own device. Backups are stored locally as database files and may optionally be protected with a password chosen by the user.

The developer has no access to backup files and does not receive any data from them. Backup passwords are not stored or transmitted and cannot be recovered.

5. Data Deletion

All data is stored locally on the device. Users can delete notes at any time from within the app.

Uninstalling the application permanently removes all locally stored data.

6. Children’s Privacy

NMP Noticer is not intended for children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect any data from children.

7. Privacy Policy Changes

Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be published with future updates of this document.
